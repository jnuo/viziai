## Key Findings

**Market:** $5-6B digital biomarkers market (2025), 19-22% CAGR. Function Health alone hit $100M ARR.

**Competition:** Mobile-first trackers are all struggling (best has 143 ratings after 6 years). Web-first is viable. No product in Turkey does what ViziAI does.

**ViziAI's wedge:** Lab-agnostic + AI PDF extraction + family profiles + Turkish support. No competitor has all four.

**Beachhead:** Dual strategy — Biohackers (#1, score 83/100) for revenue + Caregivers (#2, score 73/100) for brand story.

**Positioning:** _"Built by a son tracking his dad's health. Loved by biohackers optimizing their own."_
